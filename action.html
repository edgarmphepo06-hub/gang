About Us

The Next Level Advanced Digital Administration (NADA) was initiated at William Murray Secondary School, an official Nkhoma Synod learning institution in Malawi.

Edgar Mphepo is the CEO and Founder of NADA. He proposed the concept on 06 June 2024 and formally announced it on 18 June 2024.

Eugene Banda worked with NADA throughout its early years as an ideologist and marketing team member. He served as COO when NADA was a two‑person company, but is officially recognized as the first witness and employee. Eugene retired in July 2025, soon after finishing secondary school at William Murray.

Due to secret operations, the company attracted interest from many students who were eager to discover what NADA was all about. Edgar announced NADA to the world after leaving William Murray, following approval by the Board of Directors — consisting of departmental directors and the holding CEO — on 13 August 2025 at 15:17:09 hrs.

Today, NADA has grown into a company with a centralized structure and a strong team of employees, continuing to deliver innovative digital administration and network solutions.
