About NADA

Next Level Advanced Digital Administration (NADA) is a Malawian digital initiative founded on principles of innovation, structured governance, and technological advancement. The organization was initiated at William Murray Secondary School.

Founding History & Establishment

The concept of NADA was formally proposed by Edgar Mphepo on 06 June 2024 at 19:48:36 hrs as a student-led initiative focused on digital innovation and structured administration.

Eugene Banda was present as the first recorded witness during the initial conceptual discussions and early formation phase of the initiative.

The initiative was officially announced on 18 June 2024 at 18:30 hrs, marking its formal establishment.

Recruitment into NADA followed a structured interview process. Applicants were assessed against defined standards, and those who did not meet the criteria were formally informed of the outcome.

During its early phase, NADA operated in a controlled and discreet manner to maintain discipline, focus, and structured growth. This approach supported a selective recruitment model, ensuring only committed and capable individuals were integrated, while others contributed ideas and observed its development.

Today, NADA continues to evolve as a structured digital organization committed to delivering innovative solutions in administration, networking, and digital systems.
