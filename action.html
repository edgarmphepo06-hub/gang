Who We Are

NADA is a structured digital initiative focused on innovation and technology-driven administration systems in Malawi.

06.06.24 Founded

Founded by Edgar Mphepo

NADA (Next Level Advanced Digital Administration) was founded on 06 June 2024 at William Murray Secondary School. Eugene Banda was the first witness during formation.

Official announcement followed on 18 June 2024. Structured recruitment ensured disciplined selection and organizational integrity. Early operations were carefully controlled to ensure stability, focus, and sustainable growth.
